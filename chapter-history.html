A Brief History of Iota Phi

Iota Phi chapter was founded on February 18th, 1978 with the crossing of Thirteen Deadly Sins at Syracuse University. This dual-campus line forged a special bond between the two chapters back when both schools' Black populations were small.

Receiving its charter on September 22nd, 1979, Iota Phi became the third chapter chartered at an Ivy League institution and the seventh chapter in the state of New York. Throughout the years, Iota Phi chapter has also served men enrolled at Ithaca College and Mansfield University, positioning the Sin Chapter as a beacon of brotherhood and Black excellence in Upstate New York.
